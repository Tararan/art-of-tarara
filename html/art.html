A book cover

If you are writing a book you probably know how important it is to have a good, gripping cover. Something that will get readers’ attention and inform them what your book is all about.

There are many documented cases on importance of book covers and one of the most interesting is documented in Mark Coker’s free ebook, The Secrets to Ebook Publishing Success, where R.L. Mathewson, a romance writer, went from selling five or six copies a day of her novel, “Playing for Keeps”, to over 1,000 a day simply by updating her cover image.
